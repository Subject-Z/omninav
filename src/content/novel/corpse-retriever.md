      <p>People fear the terror of ghosts, but ghosts know the poison of human hearts.</p>
      <p>This is a traditional supernatural novel.</p>
      <p><strong>Li Zhuiyuan:</strong>The male protagonist, afflicted with a "strange illness." Exceptionally intelligent from a young age, he is deeply thoughtful and learns quickly. Before age 10, he enrolled in the gifted program at a top university in the capital, excelling among his peers. He enjoys acting childish. After his mother, Li Lan, divorced, she changed his surname to Li and sent him to live with his maternal grandparents in Nantong. An encounter with a funeral at Big Beard’s house and the Little Oriole incident sparked his interest in the supernatural, leading him to the path of "walking the shadows." He learned the skills of a corpse retriever from books found in his great-grandfather Li Sanjiang’s basement.</p>
