    <div class="english-description">
     
**Ingredients:**
- Pea shoots 150g
- White mushrooms (enoki) 80g

**Sauce:**
- Black vinegar 2 tbsp
- Light soy sauce 2 tbsp
- Toasted white sesame seeds 1 tbsp
- Sesame oil 1 tbsp
- Cooking oil 1 tbsp (for blanching)
- Salt 1/3 tsp (for blanching)

**Steps:**
1. Bring water to a boil, add 1 tbsp oil and 1/3 tsp salt. Blanch mushrooms for 30 sec over medium heat.
2. Add pea shoots, blanch for another 30 sec, then drain well.
3. Toss with vinegar, soy sauce, sesame seeds, and sesame oil. Mix well and serve.
