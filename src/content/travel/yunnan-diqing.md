    <h1 class="title">
      <span class="english-title">Diqing Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture</span>
      <span class="chinese-title">云南迪庆藏族自治州</span>
    </h1>
    <div class="content-tags">
    </div>
    <div class="english-description">

Are you seeking a sanctuary far from the everyday, a pristine land sculpted by nature's hand? Welcome to Diqing Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture in northwestern Yunnan Province – a true "Hidden Realm of Western Yunnan."

**Why Call it a "Hidden Realm"?**

- **Geographical Seclusion:** Tucked away deep within the folds of the majestic Hengduan Mountains, carved by dramatic gorges and embraced by snow-capped peaks, Diqing is like a jade jewel set amongst the clouds, seemingly untouched by the outside world.
- **Geological Wonder:** Its breathtaking landscapes encapsulate an epic 4.6 billion years of Earth's geological history, telling ancient stories at every turn.
- **Richness Beyond Shangri-La:** While many know Diqing for the famed Shangri-La, its allure extends far beyond. Though covering just 6% of Yunnan's total area, every kilometre here holds boundless wonders.

**Unmissable Natural Spectacles:**

- **The Sacred Sunrise over Meili Snow Mountain:** Picture the dawn, as the first golden threads of sunlight pierce the thin mist, gently touching the icy "skin" of Meili Snow Mountain. Watch as the sleeping giant awakens, revealing its sacred, snow-covered peaks like pristine white jade embracing the natural world.
- **Yubeng Village: A Trekker's Paradise:** Hidden deep at the eastern foot of Meili Snow Mountain lies Yubeng Village. Surrounded by mountains and shrouded in mist, this secluded haven is a destination that captures the hearts and dreams of countless hikers, a true paradise for those seeking adventure on foot.
- **Pudacuo National Park: An Ecological Masterpiece:** Located in Shangri-La City within Diqing, Pudacuo National Park sits at the heart of the "Three Parallel Rivers of Yunnan Protected Areas," a UNESCO World Natural Heritage site. It boasts a remarkably well-preserved highland inland wetland ecosystem, featuring stunning alpine lakes, marshy meadows, and subalpine coniferous forests. Like a vivid oil painting brought to life, these high-altitude lakes are complemented by meadows, forests, snow peaks, waterfalls, wetlands, and wildflowers, creating breathtaking scenes. Herds of horses and leisurely grazing yaks add a touch of vibrant life, making Pudacuo not just colourful, but truly alive.
