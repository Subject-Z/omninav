    <div class="english-description">
      <p>Don't Enter the Hollows.</p>
      <p>I know, I know. The Hollows contain Ether resources, Etherealized creatures, maybe even relics of the old civilization—all valuable treasures.</p>
      <p>But don't forget: inside, space is warped, monsters run rampant, mutations are everywhere. It's ultimately a world-devouring catastrophe, not a place for ordinary people.</p>
      <p>So, don't enter the Hollows.</p>
      <p>At least, don't enter the Hollows alone.</p>
      <p>If you insist on heading into danger, then go to New Eridu first.</p>
      <p>In that city, a real melting pot, you'll find many who need something from the Hollows: powerful corporations, gangs lurking in the streets, schemers hiding in plain sight, or ruthless officials.</p>
      <p>Get fully prepared there, find capable allies, and most importantly—</p>
      <p>Find a "Proxy."</p>
      <p>Only they have the ability to guide people out of the labyrinthine Hollows.</p>
      <p>Good luck.</p>
      <p>Zenless Zone Zero is miHoYo's brand-new 3D action game, set in a near future plagued by mysterious supernatural disasters known as "Hollows."</p>
      <p><strong>Dual Identities, Distinct Lives</strong></p>
      <p>The story takes place in this disaster-ridden world where a unique city has emerged – New Eridu. This last "oasis" has mastered the technology to coexist with the Hollows and is home to a complex web of factions. It's chaotic, bustling, dangerous, yet full of life. You will become one of the crucial few who connect the Hollows with the city, a special professional known as a "Proxy." Your story is waiting to unfold here.</p>
      <p><strong>Team Combos, High-Speed Combat</strong></p>
      <p>Zenless Zone Zero features a fluid and exhilarating combat experience. You can form a three-person squad and attack enemies using basic attacks and special skills. Dodging and parrying effectively counter enemy attacks. When an enemy enters the Stagger state, it's your chance to unleash spectacular Chain Attacks to finish them off. Notably, different enemies have unique traits, and exploiting their weaknesses is key to victory.</p>
      <p><strong>Unique Audiovisuals, Immersive Enjoyment</strong></p>
      <p>Zenless Zone Zero boasts a distinctive art style and visual design. Combined with detailed character expressions and fluid, lively animations, it helps you easily immerse yourself in the witty and humorous storyline as you begin your legendary tale. Of course, every major character needs their own theme music! Your adventures in New Eridu will be accompanied by carefully crafted soundtracks to enhance every memorable moment.</p>
      <p><strong>Diverse Factions, Surprising Encounters</strong></p>
      <p>"Random Play" can't operate without videotapes, and Proxies can't operate without Agents. In New Eridu, unexpected visitors will always be knocking at your door. Don't be fooled by cute and innocent appearances, don't be intimidated by menacing big guys, and don't look down on the fuzzy ones who shed. Why not chat with them more? Delve into their varied lives, enrich your pool of capable allies – after all, the road ahead is long, and supporting each other helps us go further.</p>
