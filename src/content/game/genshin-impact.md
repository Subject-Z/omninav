      <p>Welcome to Teyvat, a continent where the seven elements converge. Here, anyone can ascend to godhood.</p>
      <p>You arrived here from another world. In this vast land, you will journey freely, meet companions, and seek out the Seven Archons who preside over the elements, until you are reunited with your lost sibling and, at the end, witness the sum of all your travels.</p>
      <p>The Sustainer is dying; the Creator has yet to arrive. Faced with circumstances beyond their control, humanity often laments its powerlessness... But at life's harshest crossroads, should a mortal's desire reach its peak, the eyes of the gods will be cast upon them.</p>
      <p>When the lost twins reunite among the dust and sand, and the world's mysteries are revealed in the "Visions"— Traveler, where will your journey take you?</p>
      <p>Genshin Impact is a brand-new, open-world adventure RPG developed by miHoYo. In the game, you will explore a fantasy world known as Teyvat.</p>
      <p>In this vast world, you can journey across the seven nations, meet companions with diverse personalities and unique abilities, fight formidable enemies alongside them, and embark on the quest to find your lost sibling; or simply wander without a set destination, immersing yourself in this vibrant world and letting curiosity guide you to discover the mysteries hidden in every corner...</p>
