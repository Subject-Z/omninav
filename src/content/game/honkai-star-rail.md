In this galaxy, there exist beings known as **Aeons**. They shape reality, erase stars, and leave their marks upon countless **Worlds**. You, a special traveler, will journey alongside companions who follow the **Path of Trailblaze**. Aboard the Astral Express, you will traverse the galaxy, following the routes once charted by an Aeon.

Embark on an exploration of new civilizations, meet diverse companions, and unfold adventures across myriad strange and wondrous Worlds. All the answers you seek lie hidden among the stars.

So, are you ready to begin this journey of **Trailblazing**?

**Honkai: Star Rail** is an all-new space fantasy RPG developed by miHoYo.

In the game, you will board the **Astral Express**, visiting unique worlds across the universe. Team up with a diverse cast of characters to resolve crises sparked by **Stellarons** and confront the Aeons' attempts to erase civilizations.

**【Board the Astral Express, Journey Across Diverse Worlds】**

Explore a space station sealing mysterious artifacts, an alien planet locked in eternal winter, a colossal fleet hunting Abominations, a Planet of Festivities basking in perpetual dreams, and an uncharted territory where three distinct Paths converge... Every stop the Express makes reveals a never-before-seen cosmic landscape! Why is the "Room of Oddities," designed to contain countless anomalies, unable to hold *your* physical form? What secrets lie hidden at the sole location with a normal temperature on that frigid world's surface? How did the Xianzhou Alliance, masters of divination and near-magical arts, become burdened by their own longevity? In the "Perpetual Land," where destiny hangs in the balance, how will the saga of heroes unfold? Freely explore these wondrous and diverse civilizations and worlds, and unravel the mysteries behind the strange phenomena!

**【High-Quality Audiovisuals, Crafted with Care】**

Honkai: Star Rail features cinematics rendered in real-time by the game engine. A newly developed facial expression system brings characters vividly to life, complementing the high-detail environments and enhancing the overall dramatic impact. The original score, composed by miHoYo's acclaimed music team HOYO-MiX, immerses you in this interstellar adventure. You will directly confront the various anomalies and crises triggered across the galaxy by the alien Stellarons, navigating conflicts and forging alliances through numerous encounters. Make your own choices and reach the conclusion of the story!

**【Strategic Combat, Diverse Gameplay】**

Honkai: Star Rail utilizes a fresh command-based combat system that is easy to learn but strategically deep. Employ **Techniques** to gain an advantage before battle, and utilize various tactics to overcome your foes. Exploit enemy weaknesses with **Weakness Break**, trigger **Follow-Up Attacks**, apply **Damage Over Time**, and finally, unleash spectacular **Ultimates** to deliver the final blow. Beyond the thrilling turn-based battles, engage with simulation elements, relaxing mini-games, and puzzle-solving challenges. Explore a wide array of engaging gameplay and experience infinite possibilities!

**【Unique Companions, Journey Across the Stars】**

Across the sea of stars, besides yourself, countless individuals with extraordinary abilities await. You will forge bonds of camaraderie on a frozen world, battle alongside allies during the Xianzhou crisis, and experience chance meetings within golden dreams... Encounter heroes, recruit them as companions, and embark on incredible journeys together. Beyond shared adventures, you can strengthen your bonds through various interactions, share unique experiences, and uncover the hidden stories and pasts of your companions.
