      <p>During the turbulent final years of the Tang Dynasty, unrest plagues both the imperial court and the common people. The mysterious government organization known as the "Bad Guys" (Bu Liang Ren) vanishes without a trace, along with the priceless imperial treasure. Legend holds that the key to both lies within the Dragon Spring Sword, and whoever possesses it gains the power to rule the world.</p>

Years later, now a spirited young man, he leaves the mountain with his junior martial sister, Lu Linxuan, to further their training. Along the way, they befriend Ji Ruxue and Zhang Zifan. By a twist of fate, Li Xingyun unexpectedly comes into possession of the Dragon Spring Sword. News of this spreads rapidly, alerting various powerful martial arts sects who dispatch assassins to pursue Li Xingyun and his companions, aiming to seize the legendary sword.</p>
