    <div class="english-description">
      <p>On her wedding night, Liu Yunting is conscripted into the army, and Jun Yu takes his place in service. For five years, Jun Yu guards the frontier, earning great military honors. But upon returning home, she discovers her husband has slaughtered her entire family, leading to her death in anguish. Reborn at 20, Jun Yu schemes meticulously within the household to expose Liu Yunting’s true nature. With the help of the Beiliang King, she unmasks Liu Yunting and leads a victorious campaign against the Turks.</p>
