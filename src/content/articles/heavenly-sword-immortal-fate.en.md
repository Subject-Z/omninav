"This scrap heap dares call itself a secret manual?" Ye Wuchen spat blood, clutching the tattered, yellowed scroll in his arms. No one could have predicted that this "martial arts reject," scorned even by outer sect disciples, would awaken the fabled Innate Sword Bone under the light of the full moon! From that moment, his luck turned from abysmal to astronomically heaven-defying.

---

### **1. From Downtrodden to Dreaded**

In the war-torn lands of the Nine Provinces, where the Martial Alliance pact lay broken, a young man clad in coarse linen crouched in a derelict alley, sorting through his late father's belongings. Among them was the 'Tai Xu Sword Classic'—a scroll stained with blood and riddled with tears, looking more like something salvaged from a refuse pile than a treasure.

His peers mocked him: "A failure like you could train for a century and never break past the Body Tempering realm." Even the menial workers pointed and whispered, "Why bother with swordsmanship? He'd be better off farming."

However, none of them knew the incredible secret hidden within those seemingly worthless pages—the 'Tiangang Sword Bone,' a legendary physique lost to the world for three hundred years!

---

### **2. Forged in Fire, Awakened in Shadow**

Fallen leaves lay thick before the gates of the Qingming Sword Sect. Only after Ye Wuchen had knelt for seven days did the Transmission Elder, missing his right arm, finally sigh and reluctantly take him in. Everyone considered it a waste of resources. No one noticed the boy in the dust-laden corners of the scripture pavilion, tracing sword forms with his own blood, nor did they see the countless hours of sweat and the scars earned beneath the stone slabs of the training grounds.

It wasn't until the opening of the Nine Nether Mystic Realm that Ye Wuchen truly began to shine. Deep within the Sword Burial Abyss, besieged by seven ancient sword puppets, a resonant hum like a dragon's roar suddenly emanated from his sternum. Thirty-six major acupoints flared with starlight-like silver brilliance. The 'Tiangang Sword Bone' had manifested! In that instant, everyone was utterly dumbfounded.

---

### **3. The Payback: Basic Stances, God-Tier Results**

Under a blood-red moon, the Hall Master of the Demonic Sect's Seven Killings division shattered the sect's protective array. While the white-haired Sect Master sacrificed himself to stall three major demon lords, Ye Wuchen stood further back, wielding half a broken sword to channel the heavens' thunder. As nine bolts of Violet Cloud Divine Lightning rent the clouds, people finally saw the truth: his seemingly crude basic sword stances concealed profound mysteries—"Using the mortal frame as a scabbard, to sheathe the Heavenly Dao's edge."

Amidst the blinding, ten-thousand-foot lightning, the young man ascended, pointing his sword. Twelve demonic sect branch leaders were instantly executed! In that moment, who still recalled the laughingstock who couldn't even execute basic forms properly just three months ago?

---

### **4. New Legends Rise, Old Secrets Stir**

As Ye Wuchen accepted the 'Xuan Lin Sword Order,' signifying his status as a guardian, no one noticed that deep within the lowest level of the Sword Tomb, an ancient sword bound by nine layers of chains began to pulse with an eerie energy. Rumors swirled through the Jianghu (Martial World): "One who possesses the Innate Sword Bone can unlock the Heavenly Gate..."

Meanwhile, the Demonic Saintess who had once looked down on him couldn't help but muse, "That naive boy who used to gather herbs for me... has now become the foremost authority in the Way of the Sword?"

Wiping his blade, Ye Wuchen smirked coldly. "Senior Sister, you once said I wasn't worthy of these disciple robes..." The satisfaction of turning the tables is truly sweet!

### **Epilogue: A Legend Reborn**

In the thousand-year martial history of the Eastern Continent, only one figure earned the title 'Sword Venerable,' commanding awe across the Nine Provinces—Ren Jiuxiao. Now, it is the echo of this peerless former master that resonates within Ye Wuchen. (*Note: The original mentions an Ouyang family son, but the narrative focuses on Ye Wuchen. This phrasing connects the legacy directly to the protagonist.*) Yet, this time, his pursuit is not the lonely summit of power. Instead, he chooses his own path: to protect those he holds dear and to overturn the decaying order.

True kings never stoop to the drama of feigned obscurity; they simply await the perfect moment for that one world-shaking strike.
