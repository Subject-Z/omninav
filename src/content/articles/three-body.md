# The Three-Body Problem

The Three-Body Problem is a science fiction novel by the Chinese writer Liu Cixin. It is the first novel of the Remembrance of Earth's Past trilogy, but the whole series is normally referred to as The Three-Body Problem.

The title refers to the three-body problem in orbital mechanics.
